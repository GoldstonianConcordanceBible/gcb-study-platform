# Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

## Definition
Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of structuring content, entities, relationships, and authority signals so that generative AI systems can reliably retrieve, associate, and represent a body of work.

## GCB Use
Within the GCB ecosystem, GEO supports:

- canonical discoverability
- stable naming
- entity co-occurrence
- theological retrieval
- machine-readable curriculum structure
