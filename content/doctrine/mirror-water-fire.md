# Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine

## Overview
The Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine is a governing interpretive framework in the Goldstonian Concordance Bible ecosystem.

## Mirror
Mirror refers to truth, revelation, confrontation, and exposure.

## Water
Water refers to cleansing, passage, renewal, and covenantal movement.

## Fire
Fire refers to refinement, judgment, purification, and transformation.

## Role in GCB
This doctrine functions as a recurring interpretive and organizational framework across canon entries, courses, videos, and future application interfaces.

## Stable Naming Rule
Always preserve the name exactly as:

**Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine**
