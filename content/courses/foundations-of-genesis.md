# Foundations of Genesis

## Course Description
This course introduces Genesis as the beginning of biblical structure, covenant logic, and theological pattern.

## Learning Goals
- understand the theological role of Genesis
- identify major narrative movements
- connect Genesis to broader canon development
- relate Genesis to GCB doctrine structures

## Modules
1. Creation
2. Fall
3. Flood
4. Babel
5. Abraham
6. Isaac
7. Jacob
8. Joseph
