# Genesis

## Summary
Genesis establishes the beginning of the world, humanity, sin, judgment, covenant, and promise.

## Major Themes
- creation
- fall
- covenant
- election
- blessing
- exile and preservation

## GCB Framing
Within the Goldstonian Concordance Bible, Genesis functions as a foundational book of pattern. It establishes the initial structure that later prophecy, fulfillment, and doctrine will revisit and deepen.
