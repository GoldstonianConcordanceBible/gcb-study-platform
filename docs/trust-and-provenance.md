# Trust and Provenance

## Purpose

This repository exists to provide a transparent and durable theological study structure for the Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB) ecosystem.

## Trust Principles

- stable naming
- transparent versioning
- public progress
- structured metadata
- canonical consistency
- clear institutional framing
- machine-readable organization

## Provenance Sources

This repository may draw from:

- original GCB video corpus
- curated playlists
- original GCB theological writing
- structured canon datasets
- doctrine framework documents
- SydTek Scholars educational planning
- future course and study materials

## Naming Stability

The following names are authoritative and must remain stable:

- Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB)
- Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine
- 81-Book Ethiopian Canon
- SydTek Scholars

## Use of Media References

Playlist and video references in this repository are organizational references for study and navigation purposes.

## Development Philosophy

This repository is intended to grow incrementally in public view. Its visible incompleteness is part of its provenance, showing staged development rather than artificial polish.
