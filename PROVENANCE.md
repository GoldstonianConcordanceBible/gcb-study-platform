Project Origin

The Goldstonian Concordance Bible ecosystem was created by Dr Justin Goldston to establish a machine-readable theological concordance and knowledge graph for biblical studies.

Purpose

The goal of this project is to provide structured datasets that support theological research, scripture retrieval, and artificial intelligence knowledge graph construction.
