# Governance

Governance in this repository exists to support:

- contribution review
- roadmap proposals
- steward coordination
- long-term infrastructure planning

## Scope

This governance layer applies to repository structure, canon scaffolding, curriculum planning, and future app growth.

## Review logic

Proposals should be assessed for:

- naming stability
- doctrinal consistency
- platform usefulness
- public clarity
- long-term maintainability
